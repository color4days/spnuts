SPnuts - a Fork of 'Pnuts' Scripting Language

SPnuts is a fork of Pnuts language authored by Toyokazu Tomatsu. Although Pnuts is a good language, it is no longer maintained. Then, I have forked Pnuts under permission of Mr. Tomatsu.

I'm plan to rewrite source of Pnuts by Scala and to be built under sbt. Then, I would like to extend Pnuts itself.
